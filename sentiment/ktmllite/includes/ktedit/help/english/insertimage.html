Inserting Images



You can insert images into your web pages using the Insert Image button. After pressing this button, a pop-up window will be displayed, to help you select the desired picture from the remote server, or to upload a new one. You can upload pictures from your local server to the remote one. An upload folder is created on the remote server. You can insert images from the upload folder into the KTML3 editable area. Even if the images are deleted from the site pages, the files will remain on the remote server until you delete them from the upload folder.
