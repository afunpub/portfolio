Bullet List



Select several paragraphs and click on the Bullet List button. KTML3 will introduce a bullet in front of each selected paragraph. If at the end of a bulleted paragraph the "Enter" key is pressed, the next line will begin with a bullet. After applying a bullet list, the corresponding button remains activated until the list is unset. All you have to do is to position the cursor into a paragraph and check if the Bullet List button is activated or not. To unset a bullet list, you should click on the Bullet List button to deactivate it. When the “Tab” key is pressed, the paragraph is indented and the bullets change their shape.

Note: In Mozilla only, if you apply a bullet list to each paragraph separately, the spacing will increase, compared to the situation when the bullet list is applied to all selected paragraphs at once.
