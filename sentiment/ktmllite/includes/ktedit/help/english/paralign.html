Paragraph Alignment



 To help you create well-formatted pages, KTML3 allows aligning the text to the left, center, right and justified, just as the common word processors.

Left Alignment
The Align Left button will align the selected paragraph to the left margin of the page. If multiple paragraphs are selected, the command applies to all.

Center Alignment
To align the currently selected paragraph to the right of the page use the Align Center button from the KTML3 toolbar. If multiple paragraphs are selected, the command applies to all.

Right Alignment
The Align Right button will align the current paragraph to the right. If multiple paragraphs are selected, the command applies to all.

Justified Alignment
You can also choose a justified alignment (by using the Align Justify button). The paragraph will be aligned to both margins (left and right) and text will be uniformly distributed.
